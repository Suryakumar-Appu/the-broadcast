Hi There,

The Broadcast is about a signal you can almost hold. You get close, the bars climb, something almost comes through. And then it slips. Every time.

For me that feeling of almost is one of the harder parts of it all. The moments where things feel like they are working, where you can see something on the other side, and then the static comes back and you are not sure if what you saw was even real.

If you are struggling, start small, reach out to a loved one. And if a loved one sent this to you, do check in on them from time to time.

Take care :))
